Refund Policy

Last updated: August 13, 2025

1. Overview

At StarFrame Animation Studio, we are committed to delivering high-quality animation services. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be provided and the process for requesting them.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Full Refund Scenarios

A full refund may be provided in the following circumstances:

  • Project cancellation within 24 hours of deposit payment, before work has commenced
  • We are unable to deliver the agreed-upon services due to technical limitations
  • Failure to deliver the project within the agreed timeframe due to our fault
  • Breach of contract by StarFrame Animation Studio

2.2 Partial Refund Scenarios

Partial refunds may be considered when:

  • Project is cancelled after work has begun - refund based on uncompleted work
  • Mutual agreement to terminate the project due to scope changes
  • Client dissatisfaction with deliverables that cannot be resolved through revisions

3. Non-Refundable Situations

No refund will be provided in the following cases:

  • Project completion and delivery according to agreed specifications
  • Client changes requirements significantly after work has begun
  • Client fails to provide necessary materials or feedback
  • Subjective dissatisfaction with style or creative direction after approval
  • Technical issues on the client's end affecting project delivery
  • Requests for additional services not included in the original scope

4. Refund Process

4.1 Request Procedure

To request a refund, clients must:

  1. Contact us in writing within 7 business days of project completion or cancellation
  2. Provide a detailed explanation of the refund request
  3. Include relevant project documentation and communication history
  4. Allow up to 7 business days for our response

4.2 Review Process

Each refund request will be reviewed considering:

  • Project scope and deliverables
  • Communication records
  • Work completed and delivered
  • Circumstances leading to the refund request

5. Refund Timeline

Once approved, refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Credit card payments: 5-10 business days
  • Bank transfers: 3-7 business days
  • Digital wallet payments: 1-5 business days
  • International transfers: 10-15 business days

6. Revision Policy

Before considering a refund for creative dissatisfaction:

  • Each project includes up to 3 rounds of revisions
  • Revisions must be requested within the project timeline
  • Additional revisions available at standard rates
  • Major scope changes may require project re-evaluation

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  • Request a second review with additional documentation
  • Engage in mediation through a mutually agreed third party
  • Pursue resolution through applicable legal channels

8. Chargebacks

We encourage clients to contact us directly before initiating chargebacks. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in:

  • Suspension of services
  • Legal action to recover costs
  • Additional processing fees

9. Special Circumstances

9.1 Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, pandemics, or other unforeseeable circumstances, refund terms may be adjusted based on project status and mutual agreement.

9.2 Client-Initiated Changes

Significant changes to project scope may require new agreements and affect refund eligibility for work already completed.

10. Refund Calculation

When partial refunds are applicable, they will be calculated based on:

  • Percentage of work completed
  • Resources and time invested
  • Deliverables provided
  • Project milestones reached
