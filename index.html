Our Story & Philosophy

We are a small, creative, collaborative studio devoted to creating art and animation that feels alive—filled with soul, peace, and a quiet kind of magic. Our work is rooted in heartfelt storytelling, whimsical imagination, and a love for diverse voices and worlds.

We believe creativity is best when shared, and we welcome dreamers, fans, and collaborators into our warm, inclusive community. Here, every project is a journey, every world is built with care, and every creation carries its own heartbeat.

At StarFrame, we don't just create animations—we craft experiences that touch hearts and spark wonder, inviting everyone to join us on this magical journey of storytelling.

Vipshyana

Creative Director & Lead Artist

The visionary behind StarFrame's magical look and feel. Specializing in character design, illustration, and animation, Vipshyana shapes the artistic direction of every project and ensures each piece tells its own story.

Loves blending color, emotion, and motion into unforgettable visuals that touch hearts and spark imagination.

📖

Poulomi

Story & Design Lead

With a gift for weaving narratives into visuals, Poulomi develops scripts, storyboards, and designs that bring characters to life. Her knack for visual storytelling keeps each animation rooted in emotion and meaning.

Also an accomplished illustrator and animator, she co-directs creative projects alongside Vipshyana, ensuring every story resonates with its audience.

Samarth

Technical Producer & Manager

The organizational anchor of StarFrame. Samarth handles timelines, client communication, contracts, and technical workflows. From managing software to ensuring smooth deliveries, he make sure the creative team can focus on what they do best.
