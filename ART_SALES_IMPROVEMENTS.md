## 🌍 4. International Payment & Delivery Details

### Enhanced Commission Page (`commission.html`)

**Comprehensive International Section Added:**

### Payment Methods for International Clients
1. **PayPal**:
   - Instant invoicing
   - Automatic currency conversion
   - Buyer protection included
   
2. **Wire Transfer / SWIFT**:
   - For projects over $500
   - 3-5 business day processing
   
3. **Wise (TransferWise)**:
   - Lower fees
   - Real exchange rates
   - Great for regular clients
   
4. **Cryptocurrency**:
   - Bitcoin and Ethereum accepted
   - For large projects

### Currency & Pricing Information
- **Quick Currency Reference Table**:
  - ₹2,500 = ~$30 USD | ~£24 GBP | ~€28 EUR
  - ₹5,000 = ~$60 USD | ~£48 GBP | ~€56 EUR
  - ₹10,000 = ~$120 USD | ~£96 GBP | ~€112 EUR
- Explanation of exchange rate handling
- Real-time conversion in invoices

### Digital Delivery Details
- **Delivery Method**: Secure cloud links (Google Drive/Dropbox)
- **File Formats**: PNG, JPG, PDF, PSD (based on package)
- **Resolution**: Up to 6K for print quality
- **Delivery Time**: Within 24 hours of payment
- **Access Period**: 30 days (re-send available anytime)
- **Backup**: Files kept for 1 year

### Physical Prints & Shipping
**Print Options:**
- Canvas Print: ₹3,000-₹8,000 additional
- Fine Art Print: ₹1,500-₹4,000 additional
- Poster Print: ₹800-₹2,000 additional

**International Shipping:**
- Standard (15-30 days): ₹2,000-₹4,000 (~$25-$50)
- Express (7-14 days): ₹4,000-₹8,000 (~$50-$100)
- Tracking included
- Insurance available
- Customs information provided

### Time Zones & Communication
- Studio location: Varanasi, India (GMT+5:30)
- Working hours clearly stated
- Response time commitments
- Flexibility for international clients

### Legal & Licensing
**What Clients Get:**
- Full commercial usage rights
- Licensing documentation
- Detailed invoices
- Copyright explanation

**What's Not Included:**
- Reselling artwork as-is
- Creation credit claims
- NFT usage (separate agreement needed)

### International Client FAQ (6 Questions)
Addressing common concerns:
- Customs/import duties
- Exchange rate fairness
- File backup and recovery
- Payment failures
- VAT/GST handling
- Local printing options
