How to Commission Art

Commissioning custom art might seem daunting if you've never done it before. Don't worry! This step-by-step guide will walk you through the entire process, making it easy and enjoyable.

Define Your Vision

Before reaching out, take time to clarify what you want. The more specific you are, the better the result will be.

What to Consider:

  • What is the purpose? (personal, commercial, gift, etc.)
  • What style appeals to you? (realistic, cartoon, anime, abstract)
  • What's your budget range?
  • When do you need it completed?
  • What format do you need? (digital file, print, video)

Gather References

Visual references are incredibly helpful. Collect images that inspire you or show the style, mood, or elements you want.

Where to Find References:

  • Pinterest boards with similar artwork
  • Screenshots from shows or games you like
  • Photos of characters, poses, or settings
  • Color palettes or mood boards
  • Examples from our portfolio that resonate with you

Reach Out to Us

Contact us through your preferred method with your project details. We're friendly and happy to answer questions!

How to Contact:

  • Fill out our commission form (fastest response)
  • Email: starframeanimationstudio@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +91 8960356600
  • Instagram DM: @starframe.studio

Discuss & Get a Quote

We'll review your request and provide a detailed quote with timeline estimates. Feel free to ask questions or request adjustments.

What We'll Provide:

  • Detailed project breakdown
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Realistic timeline estimate
  • Revision policy explanation
  • Payment terms (50% advance, 50% on completion)

Approve & Pay Advance

Once you're happy with the quote, you'll pay the 50% advance to secure your slot. We'll send an invoice and payment details.

Payment Options:

  • UPI for Indian clients (instant)
  • PayPal for international clients
  • Bank transfer available
  • Invoice provided for all transactions

Sketching & Revisions

We'll start with initial sketches or concepts. You'll have opportunities to request changes before we move to final artwork.

Revision Process:

  • We'll share progress updates regularly
  • Review and provide feedback on sketches
  • Request changes during revision rounds
  • Approve before we proceed to color/animation

Final Approval & Payment

Once the artwork is complete, we'll send you a watermarked preview. After your approval and final payment, you'll receive the full files.

What You'll Receive:

  • High-resolution final files
  • Multiple formats (PNG, JPG, PSD, etc.)
  • Usage license documentation
  • Source files (if included in your package)
  • Certificate of authenticity for premium commissions

Enjoy Your Art!

Use your custom artwork however you planned. We'd love to see how you use it - tag us on social media!

After Delivery:

  • We're available for minor tweaks if needed
  • Share your art on social media (we love seeing it!)
  • Leave us a review to help others
  • Return for future projects (returning clients get 10% off!)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical commission take?
It depends on the complexity. Simple illustrations take 5-7 days, standard projects take 1-2 weeks, and complex animations can take 2-4 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline when we quote your project. Rush delivery is available for an additional 30% fee.
Can I use the art commercially?
Yes! Most packages include commercial rights, or you can add them for a small fee (typically ₹500-₹1000). This means you can use the artwork for business purposes, merchandise, advertising, etc. We'll provide a usage license document with your delivery.
What if I don't like the final result?
We work closely with you throughout the process with multiple checkpoints and revision rounds. You'll approve sketches before we proceed to final art. If there's still an issue, we'll work to make it right within the agreed revision policy. Client satisfaction is our priority.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. We accept PayPal, Wise, and international bank transfers. Prices are automatically converted to your currency, and we accommodate different time zones for communication.
Can I request changes after final delivery?
Minor tweaks within 7 days of delivery are usually free (typo fixes, small color adjustments). Larger changes after final approval may incur additional fees depending on the scope. We're always happy to discuss what's needed!
What's your refund policy?
Once work begins, the advance (50%) is non-refundable as it reserves your slot and covers initial work. If we can't deliver due to circumstances on our end, you'll receive a full refund. See our full refund policy for detailed terms.
Do you offer payment plans for large projects?
Yes! For projects over ₹25,000, we can arrange installment payments (e.g., 30% advance, 40% at midpoint, 30% on completion). This makes larger investments more manageable. Contact us to discuss options for your project.
Will you share my commission publicly?
Only with your permission! We love showcasing our work in our portfolio, but we always ask first. If your project is private or confidential, we'll keep it completely under wraps. Your privacy is respected.
