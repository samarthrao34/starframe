How to Commission Art

Commissioning custom art might seem daunting if you've never done it before. Don't worry! This step-by-step guide will walk you through the entire process, making it easy and enjoyable.

Define Your Vision

Before reaching out, take time to clarify what you want. The more specific you are, the better the result will be.

What to Consider:

  • What is the purpose? (personal, commercial, gift)
  • What style appeals to you? (cartoon, realistic, anime)
  • What's your budget range?
  • When do you need it completed?
  • What format do you need? (digital file, print, video)

Gather References

Visual references are incredibly helpful. Collect images that inspire you or show the style, mood, or elements you want.

Where to Find References:

  • Pinterest boards with similar artwork
  • Screenshots from shows or games you like
  • Photos of characters, poses, or settings
  • Color palettes or mood boards
  • Examples from our portfolio

Reach Out to Us

Contact us through our commission form or preferred method. We're friendly and happy to answer any questions!

How to Contact:

  • Fill out our Commission Form (Recommended)
  • Email: starframeanimationstudios@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +91 8960356600
  • Instagram DM: @starframeanimationstudios

Discuss & Get a Quote

We'll review your request and provide a detailed quote with timeline estimates. Feel free to ask questions or request adjustments.

What We'll Provide:

  • Detailed project breakdown
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Realistic timeline estimate
  • Revision policy explanation
  • Standard payment terms

Approve & Pay Advance

Once you're happy with the quote, you'll pay the 50% advance to secure your slot in our production schedule.

Payment Options:

  • UPI for Indian clients (instant)
  • PayPal for international clients
  • Direct Bank Transfer available
  • Full receipt and invoice provided

Drafting & Revisions

We'll start with initial sketches or storyboards. You'll have opportunities to request changes before we move to final art.

Revision Process:

  • Regular progress updates via email/WhatsApp
  • Provide feedback on initial sketches
  • Request changes during defined revision rounds
  • Approval required before final coloring/animation

Final Approval & Delivery

Once complete, we'll send a watermarked preview. After final payment, you'll receive all high-resolution files.

What You'll Receive:

  • High-resolution final master files
  • Multiple formats optimized for your needs
  • Usage license documentation
  • Source files (for eligible packages)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical commission take?
Complexity determines the timeline. Simple illustrations take 5-7 days, standard projects take 1-2 weeks, and complex animations take 2-4 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
Can I use the art commercially?
Yes! Most packages include commercial rights, or they can be added for a small fee. This allows you to use the art for business, merchandise, advertising, and more.
What if I don't like the final result?
We work closely with you via checkpoints and revision rounds. You approve sketches before we move to final art. If there's still an issue, we'll work to make it right within our policy.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. We accept PayPal, Wise, and international transfers. We accommodate all time zones for communication.
What's your refund policy?
Once work begins, the 50% advance is non-refundable as it reserves your slot and covers initial drafting. If we cannot deliver due to studio issues, a full refund is provided.
